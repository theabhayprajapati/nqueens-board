How to Play

  1. You will be presented with a 8x8 maze board.
  2. Your goal is to place 8 queens on the board without any of them intersecting vertically, horizontally, or diagonally.
  3. You cannot place queens on red squares.
  4. Click on a cells to place a queen on that location.
  5. You can remove a placed queen by clicking on it again.
  6. If you successfully place all 8 queens on the board, a color shower will be displayed.
  7. You can reset the game at any time by clicking on the "reset" button.

Good luck and have fun!
